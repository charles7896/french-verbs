# Privacy Policy — Entraîneur de verbes français

**Effective date:** 1 June 2026

## Overview
"Entraîneur de verbes français" (the "Extension") is a French verb conjugation
practice tool. This policy explains how the Extension handles information.

## Data we collect
**None.** The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any
personal or user data.

- It does not ask for, read, or save any personal information.
- It does not use cookies or any tracking technology.
- It does not send any information to us or to any third party.
- It does not make any network requests; it runs entirely offline on your device.
- All quiz content (verbs, tenses, questions) is bundled inside the Extension.
  Your selections and scores exist only in the page's temporary memory while you
  use the Extension and are discarded when you close it.

## Permissions
The Extension requests no special permissions that access your data, browsing
history, or activity.

## Children's privacy
Because the Extension collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for use by children.
